Welcome to St Andrews

Welcome to the St Andrews' on-line directory! Created by a loving community of people who love living, studying and working in the little town of St Andrews. We create curated content for people who are curious to know more about what goes on inside the bubble!

Discover the Hidden Gems of St Andrews

The Home of Golf

St Andrews has no less then seven stunning golf courses and hosts numerous tournaments, including the Alfred Dunhill Links Championship.

Best University in Scotland

The University of St Andrews is the oldest university in Scotland and the third oldest in the United Kingdom. It is currently ranked second in the UK by The Guardian.

Student-run Fashion Shows

St Andrews boasts multiple student-run fashion shows, spread throughout the year. Find out more about the past successes and upcoming fashion events here.

Fine Dining

From the golf greens to the cobbled highstreet, St Andrews is not short of restaurants. With cuisine from across the world, find out more about the finest dining experiences available around town.

Wildlife

Located on the East Fife Coast, St Andrews is rich in wildlife. From the herring gulls and fulmars nesting in the cliffs and the sea otters playing in the bay, to the deer that graze in the nearby fields and red squirrels that bound across the trees, there is much to see!
